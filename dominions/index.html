Arcoscephale — Order of the Oracle

"The land has long been united under the leadership of the Oracles. The Cyclopes have forged the bronze gates to the Underworld and the Oracles have held the keys."

Troops and commanders

  • Peltast: lightly armored javelin skirmishers with bucklers.
  • Light Infantry: spear-and-shield line troops, cheap with good morale.
  • Hoplite: heavier spear infantry with large shields; form the main anvil.
  • Hypaspist: elite medium infantry with high attack and precision.
  • Myrmidon: heavily armored sacred infantry with superb morale.
  • Heart Companion: sacred cavalry with lances; expensive but hard-hitting.
  • Chariot: trampling chariot for early expansion vs. light opposition.
  • Rovers: light cavalry scouts with javelins.
  • Philosopher: inexpensive researcher, no paths.
  • Priestess: sacred H1 healer for leading sacred troops.
  • Hetaire Caster (Mystic): versatile S1N1 random with chances for A/E/F/W.
  • Oracle: capital-only S2N1H2 mage-priest with random elemental paths.

Notable strengths

  • Cheap Philosopher researchers let the nation accelerate to Alteration and Construction.
  • Sacred options in both infantry (Myrmidons) and cavalry (Heart Companions) enable bless synergies.
  • Versatile mystic randoms provide early access to communions and wide battlefield magic.
  • Good starting scales and access to healers support durable armies.

Quick guide

Consider an Earth/Water-focused bless to bulk up Myrmidons and Heart Companions. Use Peltasts to screen while Hoplites and Hypaspists hold the line. Rush Alteration 3 for Body Ethereal and Iron Skin, then Construction 4 for Light of the Northern Star to supercharge Mystic communions. Site-search broadly with Oracles to secure Astral, Nature, and elemental gems.
