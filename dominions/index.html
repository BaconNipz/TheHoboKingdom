Abysia — Children of the Flame

"Abysia is a harsh, volcanic realm born from a fragment of the sun. Its people are Children of the Flame with blood that burns and bodies that radiate heat."

Troops and commanders

  • Abysian Infantry: heavy heat-aura line troops with fire resistance and bronze weapons.
  • Abysian Warrior: elite heavy infantry with magma-born strength and improved attack skill.
  • Sanguine Guard: capital-only sacred heavy infantry wielding morning stars; fire-resistant and high morale.
  • Burning One: capital-only sacred berserking infantry with high damage and a strong heat aura.
  • Salamander: elite fire-immune shock troops that spit flaming projectiles.
  • Abysian Scout: stealthy heat-resistant scout; rare for the nation’s low-map-move roster.
  • Warrior Priest: H1 sacred leader for guiding sacred infantry.
  • Warlock: capital-only F2B1 mage with astral/blood randoms; primary blood hunter and battlefield caster.
  • Anathemant Salamander: sacred F3H1 mage-priest with fire/death randoms; casts powerful fire buffs.
  • Anointed of Rhuax: capital-only sacred F2H2 leader with fire/random picks; durable prophet candidate.

Notable strengths

  • Strong capital-only sacreds (Burning Ones and Sanguine Guard) that synergize with fire blesses and Heat Aura.
  • Easy access to Blood with Warlocks, enabling rapid transition to Sabbaths, imps, and demon summons.
  • Fire-immune troops excel in heat scales and under Fire Elemental spam or Heat From Hell.
  • Heat aura punishes melee attackers and counters early-game lightly armored troops.

Quick guide

Favor Heat 3 scales and a Fire-leaning bless (e.g., Attack Skill or Reinvigoration) to maximize Burning Ones and Sanguine Guard. Expand with heavy infantry lines supported by a few Salamanders on Fireball. Research Alteration 3 for buffs like Iron Skin and Construction 4 for Flaming Arrows and Sanguine Dowsing Rods to accelerate blood hunting. Transition into imps, Fire Elementals, and later Blood summons while your heat aura troops hold the field.
